Grant writing tips
Lead with the problem — not the solution. Grant reviewers fund impact, not technology.
Quantify everything: 1,200 farmers, 94% retention, $12K MRR — numbers win grants.
Align explicitly to the funder's SDG priorities. Copy their language back to them.
Submit at least 3 days early. Late applications are auto-rejected by most grant systems.
