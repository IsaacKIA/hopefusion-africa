Data Security & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

HopeFusion Africa collects information to deliver a personalized, secure experience across our matchmaking and investment workflows. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Full name, email address, physical address, and government-issued identification files (when completing KYB/KYC checks).
  • Startup/Investor Profile Data: Company pitch deck summaries, operational metrics, fundraising goals, investment preferences, and industry tags.
  • Web3 & Blockchain Data: Public cryptographic wallet addresses (e.g., MetaMask, WalletConnect) used to interact with the Escrow smart contracts. We never collect or store your private keys.
  • Interaction Data: Transcripts generated from AI pitch deck analysis sessions, financial model projections, compliance check history, and internal team messages.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly to power core functions of the Platform, including:

  • Calculating similarity matching scores between startup profiles and active investors.
  • Running OpenAI/Claude-powered AI analysis models on your uploaded pitch summaries and financial metrics (your uploaded details are not used for public model retraining).
  • Binding user actions to active smart contract escrows on the public ledger.
  • Facilitating video mentorship calls via integrated WebRTC client sessions.

3. Data Protection Regulations

HopeFusion Africa is designed with global and localized data privacy laws in mind. We comply with data protection regulations across multiple jurisdictions, including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): Protecting data rights of European union citizens.
  • NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation): Adhering to the data collection requirements of Nigeria.
  • Kenya Data Protection Act: Protecting personal data processed in Kenya.
  • POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act): Respecting data processing regulations in South Africa.

4. Blockchain Transparency Disclaimer

Please note that transactions executed using the HopeFusion Africa Escrow contract are registered directly on a public blockchain network. The transaction sender address, contract parameters, and escrow milestone release events are permanent, public records and cannot be deleted or modified.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third-party service providers (such as cloud hosting, security verifiers, and language APIs) that are contractually bound to safeguard your information.
